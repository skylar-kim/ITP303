meet the team

rilakkuma

ceo and co-founder of rila honey

rilakkuma's favorite food in the world was honey, and wanted to bring that passion to the human market. after co-founding rila honey with barry b benson, he works hard to bring his rila honey to everyone, but remembers to relax as much as he can

barry b. benson

cfo and co-founder of rila honey

barry b benson, known for his litigation journey of suing the human race to put an exploitation to the bees, realized that his true passion is bringing the magic of honey to as many species as he can. co-founding rila honey with rilakkuma gave him a path to bring bee-approved honey to the human market.

hunny the pooh

director of quality assurance

hunny the pooh loves loves loves honey. tasting the artisanal rila honey was his dream position, as he could taste honey everyday to ensure its quality.

smokey the bear

director of security

smokey the bear, best known for his work in the wildfire prevention program, ensures that the bee colonies are properly protected from unwanted predators and humans.

skylar kim

cto

skylar kim is rilakkuma's long time friend and she loves honey for its tastiness and its medicinal properties. she's the one who made this website!
